Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PlantsBeatEnemies, published by Beigame Studio, handles information when you use the mobile game. By using the game, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

PlantsBeatEnemies does not ask you to create an account and does not directly collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise personal information.

The game stores gameplay preferences locally on your device, such as music and sound effect settings. This information is used only to keep the game working as expected.

Advertising and Analytics

The game may include third-party advertising and analytics SDKs, including mobile ad networks and measurement services. These providers may collect or process information such as:

  • Device identifiers, including the advertising identifier when permission is granted.
  • Approximate device information, operating system version, app version, language, and region.
  • Ad impressions, ad interactions, reward completion events, crash diagnostics, and gameplay-related usage events.
  • Approximate location inferred from IP address, where supported by the provider.

This information is used to deliver ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, improve stability, and understand basic app usage. Third-party providers process data under their own privacy policies.

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS, the game may request permission to use the device advertising identifier. If you decline, the game will still be playable, but ads may be less relevant. You can change tracking permissions in the iOS Settings app.

Rewarded Ads

PlantsBeatEnemies may offer rewarded ads that let you revive during a game. A reward is granted only after the advertising provider reports that the ad was completed successfully.

Children's Privacy

The game is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the game, please contact us so we can review the issue.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with advertising, analytics, security, and platform service providers only as needed to operate the game, provide ads, measure performance, comply with law, or protect the app from abuse.

Data Retention

Local gameplay preferences remain on your device until you delete the app or reset the settings. Data processed by third-party providers is retained according to their own policies.

Your Choices

  • You can disable music and sound effects inside the game.
  • You can limit ad tracking through iOS privacy settings.
  • You can delete the app to remove local game data stored on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact Beigame Studio at support@beigamestudio.com.
